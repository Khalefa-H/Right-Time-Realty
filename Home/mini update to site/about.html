About Us

Sierra Fields - Founder, CEO

The history on why the company got started. During 2018 my family and I became homeless and lost everything. Once we got back on our feet with having to rebuild everything back up like credit, finance, and home to call our own! May 2019 Right Time Realty was founded as a result of starting the company aiming to assist 1st time home buyers' stepping into their new build home! I am Sierra Fields CEO of Right Time Realty.

Our Mission

Here at Right Time Realty LLC our goal is to assist 1st time homebuyers in purchasing their 1st new built home from dirt to glory!


We will provide realtors, and staff the tools, support, training, continuing education, and a safe work environment in-person or virtually all over the nation. We enhance our creativity to bring forth knowledge and well-being to our clients!
