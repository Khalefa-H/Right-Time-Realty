About Us

Meet Sierra Fields, the driving force behind Right Time Realty. With a personal history of overcoming homelessness and rebuilding her life, Sierra understands the importance of finding a place to call home. As the CEO, she founded Right Time Realty in 2019 with a clear vision: to empower first-time home buyers on their journey to homeownership. Sierra's commitment to exceptional service and her dedication to helping others make her the ideal partner in your real estate journey. Trust Sierra and the Right Time Realty team to guide you every step of the way and find the perfect home that fits your needs.
